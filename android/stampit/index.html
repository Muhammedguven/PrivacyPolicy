1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the mobile application Stamp It, developed and published by Kolapps, handles information. Stamp It is a stamp creation app that lets you design and manage stamps; images and related data are stored on your device using local storage and a local database.

App page: Google Play Store

2. Personal Data

Stamp It does not collect, transmit, or process personal data. There is no account registration, login, or user profile. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other identifiers for use of the app.

3. Data on Your Device

Stamps, images, and app preferences you create or choose are stored only on your device (for example in local database and file storage managed by the app). This data remains under your control and is not uploaded to our servers by the app for app functionality.

4. Network and Third-Party Services

Stamp It does not send your stamp images or local app data to Kolapps for normal app use. The app does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that collect personal data. Your content stays on the device unless you yourself share it outside the app (for example via another app or system share sheet).

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 in a way that requires collecting personal information, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through Stamp It.

6. Security

Data stored on your device is protected by your device’s operating system and any security settings you use. Uninstalling the app may remove locally stored data according to your device and system behavior; you may wish to export or back up content before removal if your device allows it.

7. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
