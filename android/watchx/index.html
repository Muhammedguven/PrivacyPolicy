1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the mobile application WatchX, developed and published by Kolapps ("we", "us"), handles information when you use the app. WatchX helps you follow public posts from accounts you choose.

Google Play: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.kolapps.watchx

An iOS release may be added later; this policy will apply to that version as well.

2. Information We Process

We do not use analytics products to track how you use the app, and we do not ask you for unrelated personal details. To run the service, we process only what is needed:

  • Account handle. You provide a public username (handle) associated with your use of the app so we can identify your session and personalize your feed.
  • App data on your devices. The app keeps information on your device such as sign-in state, preferences, and the list of accounts you follow, so the app can work offline where possible and resume where you left off.
  • Watch pairing (optional). If you use a companion watch app, information you already have in the phone app (such as your following list and display preferences) may be transferred between your paired devices so both stay in sync.
  • Subscriptions. If you buy a subscription, payment is handled by your app store. We receive only what is necessary to know that your subscription is active.
  • Service operation. When you use online features, our servers process the handle and technical data required to deliver content, maintain security, and reduce abuse.

We do not collect separate analytics datasets about your behavior beyond what is described here.

3. Public Content

The app displays posts and profile details that are already publicly available from third-party platforms. We do not control those platforms' own privacy practices.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only where needed to operate the app (for example, with infrastructure that hosts our service and with app store providers for billing), or when required by law.

5. Retention and Your Choices

You can stop using the app at any time. To request deletion of information we hold in connection with your use of WatchX, email us at mguvenn96@gmail.com. We will respond in line with applicable law.

6. Children's Privacy

WatchX is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have received such information, please contact us.

7. International Users

Depending on where you live, your information may be processed in countries other than your own. If you have questions, contact us at the email above.
